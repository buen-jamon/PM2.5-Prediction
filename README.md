# PM2.5 Prediction
This projects takes wather data from 10 diferent stations in Guadalajara metropolitan area and using CRISP-DM Methodology determinate the main factors that contribute to the PM2.5 measurements

PM2.5 Stands for microscopic particulate matter that is 2.5 micrometers or less in diameter, just to dimension it, a human hair is between 40µm and 120µm, this particles are 16 to 48 times thiner than a human hair. This represent a thread to our health so that these particles are so small that can get through our natural defenses ending into our lungs and bloodstream causing respiratory, cardiovascular and neurogical side effects. In long term this can lead to damages blood vessels and promotes plaque buildup, increasing the risk of heart attacks, strokes, asthma, bronchitis and it´s an important risk factor for lung cancer. In some cases, these particles get into  our brain, causing inflamation and increasing risk of Alzheimer, Dementia and other neurodegenerative disorders.
